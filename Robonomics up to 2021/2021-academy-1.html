Welcome to the Robonomics Academy!
This is the start of your journey through the world of Web3 technologies. Get ready for entering a community of learners and expanding your knowledge of Robotics & IoT.
Robonomics allows using robots as autonomous agents that receive commands from a human or another robot and do some useful work, storing a report of their actions in Blockchain. The platform can be seen as a communication layer in ROS that dispatches the robots when the customer pays.
