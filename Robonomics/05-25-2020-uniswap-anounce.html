UNISWAP IS MUCH MORE INTERESTING THAN EXCHANGES
If we talk about Robonomics, then uniswap gives an initial impulse of liquidity in a fully automatic and autonomous mode - this is a smart contract in an Ethereum computer. The Uniswap trading algorithm controls the increase or decrease in price. The key parameter for calculating the current price for the user is the liquidity pool, or rather the ratio of the desired quantity to the exchange (swap) divided by the liquidity pool. If the liquidity pool is too small, then each purchase or sale will greatly shift the price from the current rate.
