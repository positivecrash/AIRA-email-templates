ROBONOMICS 2018: BLOCKCHAIN FOR SMART CITY

Airalab team invites you to take part in the summer school on smart cities.
STAGE "WE ARE BUILDING" FOR DEVELOPERS AND EVERYONE INTERESTED IN THE TECHNICAL PART
August 20 to 24 we invite you to create a simulation of the Smart City together with us. We will listen to lectures and assemble prototypes, solve cases and test MVPs, rack our brains and take rest. Russian and international experts will help us in that. They will work with us all the time and some will come for just one day to share their experience.
Level of your competences to qualify for the summer school:
If you can install the Ethereum client, then this is already enough for us. We will form groups with different requirements, so your interest in the subject is what we really need.
Venue of the event:
Technopark "Zhigulevskaya valley" in Togliatti
How to participate:
Event page on Timepad
STAGE “WE ARE TESTING” FOR EVERYONE INTERESTED IN THE FUTURE OF SMART CITIES AND APPLICATION OF BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGIES
August 24 to 26 we will deploy a simulation of the smart city on the picturesque bank of the Volga river. Everyone will be able to take part in testing, discussing and criticizing the solutions applied in it. Skeptics and romantics, opponents and supporters, theoreticians and engineers - all of you are invited to express and prove your stance.
Venue of the event:
Holiday resort “Lada”
