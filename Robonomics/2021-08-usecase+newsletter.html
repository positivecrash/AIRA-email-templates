Robot the artist Gaka-Chu
One of the most memorable Robonomics use cases — a retired welding robot Gaka-Chu that has become an artist.
Gaka-Chu is controlled by the Ethereum blockchain and has its own crypto wallet. The robot is buying art supplies and paying electricity bills with the money from its sold artwork.
